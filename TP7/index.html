Im a graphic & web designer living in Montevideo, Uruguay.
I love every step in conception of a design, from the initial creative process, through the final deployment of a project.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

I have a passion for typography and lettering, creating hand drawn custom logo types, t-shirts designs, bespoke magazine headers, prints and posters. This all springs from learning the wonderful craft of calligraphy. It's amazing what can be achieved when we make stuff with our hands.

  • 1. Photoshop
  • 2. Illustrator
  • 3. Indesign


WEB DESIGN

I design clean, elegant and friendly websites built with web standards. That means I’m strict with accessibility and usability best practices in order to make my work web friendly.

  • 1. HTML
  • 2. CSS
  • 3. SASS
  • 4. Bootstrap
  • 5. Responsive
My function is to create an appropriate combination between design and usability to help you to communicate your message, develope your corporate identity and create an original web concept to improve you on-line presence.
